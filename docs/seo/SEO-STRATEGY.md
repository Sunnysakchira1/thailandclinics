## Positioning

**Tagline for E-E-A-T copy:** "We review the data. You choose the clinic."

ThailandClinics does not pick sides or accept payment for placement. Rankings are based entirely on verified Google data (rating × review count). AI-generated summaries are disclosed as AI-generated, based on verified patient reviews.

This positions us as **infrastructure** — not a lead-gen affiliate like CleverThai or a booking platform like DentalDepartures. Trust is the product.
